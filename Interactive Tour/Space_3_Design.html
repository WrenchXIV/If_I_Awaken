If I Awaken in Los Angeles · Space 03 of 06

Five Asian
Neighborhoods in one breath

A wide arc of five elevated stages, one per enclave — Chinatown, Little Tokyo, Koreatown, Historic Filipinotown, Thai Town — each fronted by its real-life gateway monument. One rapper-poet moves the song along the arc; five resident dancers vocalize the chorus from their stations.

Chinatown · Little Tokyo · Koreatown · Historic Filipinotown · Thai Town

An arc of five small elevated stages fans around a central audience gathering space. One resident dancer stands on each stage throughout the entire section. A rapper-poet performs each verse from the town that verse is about, then travels along the arc to the next stage during each chorus, arriving just in time to land the next verse. Lighting isolates the active town during verses; all five light up during choruses, with accent flickers firing on each town as the lyrics name it.

No walls divide the zones. Instead, each neighborhood gets its own scenic landmark — specific picks TBD by the writer. Two of them are functionally locked in: the audience enters Space 3 by walking through the Chinatown landmark (at the first town of the song) and exits through the Thai Town landmark (at the last). Each end of the arc becomes a threshold into and out of a town.
