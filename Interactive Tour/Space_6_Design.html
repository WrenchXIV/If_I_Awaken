If I Awaken in Los Angeles · Space 06 of 06

The Stage — a classical finale

After five immersive neighborhood environments, audiences arrive at the only space in the show that looks like a theater. A classical proscenium — matte black frame, warm walnut deck, black velour with a white scrim reveal — sits inside a raw warehouse shell. The tour guide opens solo; the company gathers beat by beat; a full-ensemble finale lands before a flat-line bow.

Amphitheater bowl · Classical proscenium · Scrim reveal · Full-company bow

Audience exits Space 5 through the NE corner and ramps down into the mid-house cross-aisle of the amphitheater. Three rows descend ahead of them toward the stage (R1 at –2 ft, in the old dock pit); five rows ascend behind them to R8 at +32". Every seat is within ~30 ft of the stage edge. The stage itself is deliberately neutral — a matte black proscenium wraps the existing steel columns into a 44 × 24 ft portal, the deck is warm walnut hardwood raised 2 ft over the dock pit, a full-width black velour traveler closes the back with a white bobbinet scrim waiting behind it for the finale. Nothing on the stage references LA or any earlier space.

The design move is the contrast: a pristine classical stage sitting inside a raw brick warehouse, brick walls left exposed on both audience sides. The seven-beat arc runs tour guide solo → band arrives → poets arrive → dancers arrive → final number (velour opens, scrim ignites) → bow. The scrim reveal is Space 6's only capital-R reveal — everything else is restraint so that reveal can land.

02Audience perspective
Two moments from the center of the house
VIEW A · PRE-SHOW VELOUR CLOSED · HOUSE WARM

Audience arrives. A classical theatrical stage sits inside a raw brick warehouse — matte black proscenium wrapping the existing steel columns, warm walnut deck glowing under house light, black velour traveler closed across the full back wall. Nothing on the stage is doing anything. The warehouse brick around it is doing all the talking.

VIEW B · FINALE VELOUR OPEN · SCRIM IGNITED

The climactic moment. Velour parts to the sides; the white scrim ignites with light from behind — deep, luminous, seemingly infinite. Full company silhouetted against the glow: band row upstage, tour guide and poets mid, dancers downstage in dynamic pose. The only capital-R reveal of Space 6. Everything else has been restraint so this moment could land.

✓ Locked

  • Seating — Option B amphitheater bowl, mid-house entry, 3 rows descending + 5 rows ascending
  • Stage — 45 × 19 ft at +2 ft, built over dock pit, 6 ft under-stage clear for crossover
  • Proscenium — matte black frame wrapping existing steel columns, 44 × 24 ft opening
  • Stage deck — warm walnut hardwood, matte/satin finish
  • Back-wall system — black velour traveler (12 ft, motorized) + white bobbinet scrim behind for finale reveal
  • Side masking — standard black legs SL and SR (3 pairs, 14 ft)
  • House walls — raw brick stays exposed and untreated on both audience sides
  • Roller door treatment — covered by scenic back wall, not used as show element
  • Beat arc — 7 beats from tour-guide solo through full-company bow; scrim reveal is the single capital-R reveal
  • Capacity — 152 positions: 144 fixed + 4 WC + 4 companion at R5 upper and R8 back
  • Entry/exit — SW corner ramp from Space 5 to mid-house cross-aisle; rear walkway egress after bow
