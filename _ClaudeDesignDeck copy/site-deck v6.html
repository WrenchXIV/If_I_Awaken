A Cultural Anchor

A permanent home for the stories LA tells about itself — built in the Arts District, owned by the city, not exported back to it. The first show of its kind to put the real Los Angeles at the center, every night.

12 shows a week, 60 weeks a year, drawing audiences from across the basin and beyond. Jobs for actors, musicians, dancers, technicians — and a reason for visitors to stay an extra night.
